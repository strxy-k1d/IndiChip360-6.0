Prototypes

E-Waste Recovery AI

Vision + classifier to pick valuable components.

Model: YOLO-based sorter • Accuracy: ~94% (prototype) • Integrates with robotic arms and conveyor indexing for high throughput recovery.

AI Chip Tester

Automated diagnostics & anomaly detection.

Features: waveform analysis, automated acceptance reports, customizable test scripts, integration with CI pipelines for silicon validation.

RISC-V FPGA Demo

Open cores tailored for local workloads.

Demo: Multicore RISC-V on FPGA • Toolchain: open-source flows • Use-cases: edge-AI, industrial controllers, education kits.
